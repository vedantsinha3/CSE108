Basic Rules

Here are foundational rules you’ll see at most levels of play.

  1. Each team has five players on the court.
  2. Dribble or pass to move the ball; no running with the ball.
  3. Field goals are worth two points inside the arc and three points behind it.
  4. Personal fouls involve illegal physical contact.
  5. The shot clock limits time to attempt a shot.
  6. After a made basket, the opposing team inbounds the ball.
