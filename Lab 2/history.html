History of the Game

Basketball began in the late 19th century and has evolved through changes to equipment, rules, and style of play, spreading worldwide thanks to professional and international competition.

Milestones

  • Creation as an indoor winter sport.
  • Introduction of the dribble and shot clock.
  • Three-point line adoption era.
  • Global growth through international tournaments.
